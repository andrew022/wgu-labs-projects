Missouri is located in the midwest of the United States. The states was admitted to the Union on August 10th, 1821. Missouri was the 24th state added. Missouri has a population around 6.1 million which ranks them around 19th in the country. The average income of a resident in Missouri sits around $53,000. Majority of Missouri residents live in urban areas while only 33% live in rural areas. To learn about some of the citites in Missouri, click on the links to the left of the page.
